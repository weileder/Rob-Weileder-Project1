BERT'S PIZZERIA


Bert's Pizzeria was established in 2004, in Wauwatosa, WI. In a little shop down in the heart of the village, Bert Hendrick and his wife, Maggie, opened up a little one oven pizzeria. On the day of their grand opening, they only had a few tables and a short counter for their guests to sit and enjoy the food. As they celebrate their 15th anniversary, they have enjoyed all these years filling their customers stomach with delicious food and filling their hearts with joy!
